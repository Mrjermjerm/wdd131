Symphony Orchestra

On October 30th, Norman Krieger performed two of George Gershwin's most influential works in the Snow Building's concert hall, celebrating the 100th anniversary of Rhapsody in Blue.

Speedy Spud 5K & Mashed Potato Mile

On October 12th, Rexburg held two new races, the Speedy Spud 5K and Mashed Potato Mile, for adults and children respectively. The race began and ended by the pickleball courts, and all participants received a T-shirt.
